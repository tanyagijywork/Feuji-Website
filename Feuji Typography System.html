Button System

Three tiers × two backgrounds. Capsule shape — border-radius: 999px. Typography token: DM Sans 400 16/18 0. Icon slot is optional — use 16×16 SVG icons only. Orange Primary replaces Black Primary in all contexts where brand colour is intentional.
